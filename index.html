His Story

Tom Misch is a singer, songwriter, and a producer from London England who has been rocking the UK music scene and his music has reached different places around the world.

Misch started producing beats in his bedroom and uploading it on SoundCloud, where he instantly became a celebrity of the platform. From then on, he continued to pursue his music and released multiple albums collaborating with different big artists.

The Sound of Tom Misch

Misch has definitely created his own unique sound by the influence of the many artists that he looks up to whether be it a jazz musician or a fellow producer. His music is heavily influenced by Bill Evans, John Mayer, Kaytranada, Nirvana, J Dilla, The Soulquarians, and many more.
